# ❤️ Mood Support — Addon

User shares their feelings → bot responds with comforting Quranic verses.

**Commands:** `/mood`, `/mood sad`, `/mood anxious`
**Mood mapping:**
- Sad → Comfort & Grief category
- Anxious → Anxiety & Stress category
- Angry → Patience & Forgiveness
- Lonely → Faith & Family
- Grateful → Gratitude category
- Hopeful → Guidance category
